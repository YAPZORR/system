Our System

Student Information System (SIS)

An online application that allows the student to access all their academic records such as grades, billing and finances, subject schedule and offerings. The student must also evaluate their faculties from the previous semester before they can preview their grades. The students are also able to apply for a scholarship grant listed by the scholarship officers.

Admission Information System (AIS)

This online system serves as the portal for registration to all aspiring students who wish to apply for college. The Program Chairpersons will assess the submitted educational background of the applicants and place them according to their qualified programs.

Online Grade Submission(OGS)

A web application that provides a facility for ASSCAT faculties to submit their grade sheets via web. The Deans from respective Colleges are responsible for the approval of the submitted grade sheets from the faculties under them. In addition, the application has a dashboard displaying the summary of approved grade sheets for the Vice President for Academic Affairs, Registrar and College President.
